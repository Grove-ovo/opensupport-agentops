Fit

Best for support AI teams near real automation.

Use this sprint when an AI agent can affect customer trust, refunds, credits, account actions, escalation, or private customer context. The first rollout mode can be shadow, assist, or low-risk auto after the replay gates are clear.

It is not a broad transformation project. It is one workflow, one owner, one reviewer, and a concrete acceptance suite.

Data handling

Designed for sanitized workflow evidence.

The default sprint does not require production credentials, raw private customer data, or administrator access. Useful inputs are approved policies, sanitized workflow notes, anonymized transcript excerpts, screenshots, current eval examples, and reviewer judgment.

The working artifacts focus on acceptance criteria: replay cases, scorecard rows, action boundaries, escalation gates, and known gaps. Private customer data stays out unless a separate written agreement and transfer method are approved.
