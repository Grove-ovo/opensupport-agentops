Data handling notice

Sanitized inputs by default.

This notice describes the default working model for the CX AI Agent Reliability Sprint. It is not a substitute for a client-specific legal agreement, DPA, NDA, or security review.

Default Inputs

The sprint is designed around approved policies, public or sanitized documentation, anonymized transcript excerpts, screenshots, current eval examples, workflow notes, and reviewer judgment. Production credentials and raw private customer data are not required for the default assessment.

Sensitive Data

Do not send raw credentials, payment data, secrets, private keys, full customer records, regulated health data, or legal case data unless a separate written agreement and transfer method are approved first.

Use Of Materials

Client-provided materials are used to produce the selected workflow map, threat model, replay-test specifications, scorecard, implementation path, and known-gaps summary.

Retention

Delivery artifacts are retained only as needed to complete the sprint, support acceptance, and handle billing records. A client can request deletion of working copies after final acceptance, except where records must be retained for payment, tax, or dispute documentation.

Third Parties

The default workflow does not require uploading client private data to new third-party systems. If a specific tool, payment processor, or file-transfer route is needed, it should be confirmed in writing before use.
